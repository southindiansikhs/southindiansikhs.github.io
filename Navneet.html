NAVNEET KAUR

Navneet Kaur is an Indian field hockey player who plays as a forward for the Indian national team. She has represented India in 100 international matches. On April 8, 2022, she played her 100th international match in the 2021-22 Women's FIH Pro League match against the Netherlands team. She was awarded the Player of the Match for her outstanding performance in the game. Kaur is currently employed with the Western Railway division of the Indian Railways.











ACHIEVEMENTS

Navneet Kaur has represented India in numerous international field hockey tournaments, including the Commonwealth Games and the Asian Games. She was a part of the Indian team that won a silver medal at the 2018 Asian Games in Jakarta, Indonesia. She also played a crucial role in helping India win a bronze medal at the 2017 Women's Hockey Asia Cup held in Japan.

In addition to her international achievements, Navneet has also had success in domestic field hockey. She has won several national championships with her club team, the Western Railway, and was named the best forward at the 2019 Senior National Hockey Championship.

Navneet's 100th international cap is a significant milestone in her career, highlighting her commitment and dedication to the sport. Her exceptional performance in the match against the Netherlands team, where she won the Player of the Match award, is a testament to her skill and talent as a forward in field hockey.

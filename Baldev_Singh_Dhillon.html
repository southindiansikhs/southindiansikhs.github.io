BALDEV SINGH DHILLON

Baldev Singh Dhillon is a globally recognized agricultural scientist who served as the Vice Chancellor of Punjab Agricultural University in India from 2011 to 2021.

Baldev Singh Dhillon is a well-known agricultural scientist with an international reputation, who served as the Vice Chancellor of Punjab Agricultural University in India from 2011 to 2021. Prior to that, he held various prestigious positions such as Assistant Director General at ICAR, Director of NBPGR (ICAR), Director of Research at Punjab Agricultural University and Guru Nanak Dev University. He also worked at the University of Hohenheim in Stuttgart, Germany, and the International Maize and Wheat Improvement Centre in Mexico. In addition, he worked on maize breeding, genetics, and biotechnology at the University of Birmingham in the UK.

During Dhillon's tenure as Vice Chancellor of Punjab Agricultural University, the institution achieved numerous accolades, including being ranked as the 'Best Agricultural University' and the 'Third Best Agricultural Research Institute' in India by ICAR in 2017, and being ranked 'First in Punjab' and the 'Second Best Agricultural University in India' by the Union Ministry of Human Resource Development in the same year. PAU also received recognition for its research articles and citations in 2017 by CII-Indian Citation Index, and was ranked second in India and 232nd in the world for scientific papers for world universities by National Taiwan University in the same year. The Indian Society of Genetics and Plant Breeding also honoured PAU for its landmark varieties, and the leading magazine India Today recognized PAU as one of the 70 Icons of Modern India in its special issue on 70 years of Independence.

Dhillon was born in Amritsar in 1947 and earned his B.Sc. in agriculture from Khalsa College, Amritsar, his postgraduate degree M.Sc. from Punjab Agricultural University, and his doctorate from the Indian Agricultural Research Institute (IARI) in New Delhi. He has published over 350 research publications and several books, and is renowned for his scientific breakthroughs in plant breeding.
